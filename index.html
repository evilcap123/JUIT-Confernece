About the Conference

The International Conference on AI & Intelligent Materials for Sustainability and Climate-resilient Construction (AIM-SCC 2026) brings together global experts, academicians, researchers, policymakers, and industry innovators to advance the future of infrastructure design, construction, and climate adaptation.

As climate hazards intensify across the world and infrastructure demands reach unprecedented levels, the convergence of Artificial Intelligence, Intelligent/Smart Materials, and Sustainable Construction Innovations has emerged as the next transformational leap in engineering.

AIM-SCC 2026 is designed as a premier interdisciplinary platform, enabling knowledge exchange, scientific collaboration, and the exploration of frontier technologies. This conference will highlight the integration of machine learning, digital twins, automation, advanced materials, and nature-based solutions in constructing resilient, sustainable, and smart infrastructure systems.

The event emphasizes bridging the gap between research and practice, offering participants exposure to emerging tools, validated field applications, and global case studies. With contributions expected from leading universities, research institutions, industries, and government agencies, AIM-SCC 2026 aims to become a defining event shaping the future of sustainable and climate-ready civil infrastructure.
